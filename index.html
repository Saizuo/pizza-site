About Pizza Care

Nestled uptown in Pittsburgh, Pizza Care has been serving delicious and healthy pizzas for over a decade. We're committed to using only the freshest ingredients, with options like gluten-free crusts and vegan toppings. From mouthwatering pizzas to crispy wings, our diverse menu caters to all tastes. Come taste the care at Pizza Care, where great food meets healthy choices.
